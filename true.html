Is it possible to use content I have downloaded for future projects for clients?

Yes, it is possible. If you become an Up Premium User, all of the content you are downloading automatically comes with a commercial license.

However, if you remain an Up Free User, you need to make sure you are purchasing/downloading the content with the commercial license option. Read more on our license options here.

What advantages will I gain from Up Premium?

For only $9.99/month or $89.99/year, you will gain access to over 1,000 high-quality premium resources that you no longer have to provide author accreditation for. You will also be able to enjoy unlimited downloads for free resources, an AD-free experience while browsing, and the perk of Premium Support.

Is there automatic renewal for my subscription?

Yes! Unless you cancel your subscription on your member profile before the new subscription period begins, the subscription you have chosen will automatically renew (at the same price).

What are the main differences between Up Free and Up Premium?

Up Free
  • Limit of 3 free downloads a day
  • Required author accreditation
  • Can only download premium resources by paying a price set by the seller
  • Ads
Up Premium
  • Can download unlimited amount of free resources
  • No need for author accreditation; all resources can be used for commercial projects
  • Can download premium resources
  • No ads

What is Premium Support?

Premium Support is guaranteed lifetime support from the user you are downloading a resource from. With Premium Support, you will be guaranteed a response within 24 hours. The Premium Seller is responsible for providing Premium Users with Premium Support.

Can I cancel my plan and order a refund of my purchase?

You can cancel your plan at any time however, once you have purchased a Premium Membership, you may not request a refund.
